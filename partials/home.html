JIGSAW!

Did you know that a London cartographer, John Spilsbury, is credited with commercializing jigsaw puzzles back around 1760? Neither did I until recently. They were apparently originally known as dissections - pretty disappointing that that name didn't stick around with them...

Join in with this enduring pastime by clicking the button below - simply drag and drop the pieces to where you think is correct, then hit check when you think you've got it!
